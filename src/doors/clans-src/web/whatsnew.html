v0.97b2 — Date TBD

Completed the Full Quest Campaign

The original game shipped with 6 quests out of a planned 20. All 14 missing quests have been written, forming a full campaign arc:

  • New standalone quests:
    • Drakuul's Legacy — destroy a vampire in his crypt (Very Hard)
    • The Quest for the Lost Knight — rescue a knight from an abandoned keep
    • The Quest for Vengeance — track down a bandit captain for the beggar
    • The Spirits of Lovers — enter the cave from the Lady's ghost story
    • The Lost Treasure — claim the 3000 gold treasure the old man spoke of
    • The Poet's Quill — recover the poet's stolen golden quill
    • The Spirit World — light four candles and face the demon Diablo
  • Orc chain (Q1 → Q11 → Q15):
    • The Orc Lord — assault the orc lord's fortress deep in the mines
  • Five Great Evils chain (Q15 → Q14 → Q16 → Q17):
    • The Wyvern Lord — one of the Five Great Evils
    • The Dragon Lord — one of the Five Great Evils
    • The Demon Lord — the last of the four lesser evils
  • Endgame chain (Q17 → Q7 → Q18 → Q20):
    • The Quest for the Lost King — rescue King Claudius from the dungeon
    • Sword of The Heavens — recover the legendary Heavenly Sword
    • The Dark One — the campaign finale; destroy the fifth Great Evil
  • New NPC content: King Claudius is now a full NPC you meet during the Lost King quest. The Common Man reveals the Five Great Evils chain. The Beggar, Jester, and Acolyte have new quest-giving topics. Existing NPCs (Knight, Village Idiot, Lady, Old Man, Psychic, Paladin, Poet) now reveal their quests as originally intended.
  • New item: the Heavenly Sword, the most powerful weapon in the game, awarded by the Sword of The Heavens quest.
  • 41 new monsters added for the quest encounters.
