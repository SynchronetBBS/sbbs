TOWN HALL

[12.1]

Overview

The town hall is the center of village government. What you can do here depends on whether your clan rules the village or not. All players can vote, deposit gold, and view the village empire. The ruler has additional powers to build structures, set taxes, and manage the village.

[12.2]

Citizen Menu

If you are not the ruler, the town hall commands are:

KeyAction
DDeposit gold into the village vault.
EDonate resources to the village empire.
WWrite a message to the ruler.
BVoting booth -- cast your vote for the next ruler.
SEmpire stats -- view the village empire's statistics.
PPublic discussion board.
HHelp for citizens.
VView your clan stats.
/Chat with villagers in the town hall.
QReturn to the main menu.
[12.3]

Ruler Menu

If your clan rules the village, you have these commands:

KeyAction
SStructures -- build and upgrade village buildings.
EEconomics -- set tax rates and manage the vault.
PManage empire -- edit the village empire (troops, buildings).
RConscription rate -- set the military draft rate (once per day, 0-20%).
CColors -- change the village's color scheme.
LFlag -- customize the village flag colors.
MMake announcement -- broadcast a message to all players.
BVoting booth -- view current election standings.
DPublic discussion board.
TToggle empire stats -- show or hide empire info for citizens.
HHelp for rulers.
VView your clan stats.
!Abdicate -- give up rule of the village.
/Chat with villagers in the town hall.
QReturn to the main menu.
[12.4]

Structures

The ruler can build and upgrade village structures. Each structure unlocks or improves a service available to all players. Building costs gold from the village vault.

KeyStructurePurpose
CChurchHealing, blessings, resurrections.
TTraining HallMember training and recruitment.
PPawn ShopUsed item trading.
ZWizard's ShopScrolls, books, and item identification.
FSmithyImproves weapon and armor quality at market.

Each structure has multiple levels. Higher levels unlock better services and items. Check the vault (S in the build menu) to see how much gold is available before building.

[12.5]

Economics

The ruler controls the village economy through the economics menu:

KeyAction
TTax rate (0-50%, once per day). Income tax on player earnings.
GGST (0-50%, once per day). Goods and sales tax on market purchases.
DDeposit clan gold into the village vault.
WWithdraw gold from the village vault (up to 3 times per day).

Higher tax rates generate more revenue for the village but make the village less attractive to players. A wise ruler balances income with player satisfaction.

The village vault funds all building construction and other village expenses. Both the ruler and citizens can deposit gold, but only the ruler can withdraw.

[12.6]

Voting

All players can vote for who should rule the village. Press B in the town hall to enter the voting booth.

KeyAction
CChange your vote. Select a different clan as your choice for ruler.
VView stats.
QLeave the voting booth.

The voting booth displays the top 10 candidates and their vote counts. The clan with the most votes when the current ruler abdicates or is ousted becomes the new ruler.

[12.7]

Abdication

A ruler can voluntarily give up control of the village by pressing ! in the town hall. This resets the village to having no ruler. The clan that abdicated loses 100 points.

Warning: Abdication should not be taken lightly. You will lose 100 points and the village will be left without a ruler until a new one is elected.
