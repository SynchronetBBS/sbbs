Deleting Your Clan

Press ! from the main menu to permanently delete your clan. You will be asked to confirm this choice.

WARNING: Once deleted, your clan, all its members, items, gold, empire, and alliance memberships are gone forever. This cannot be undone.
[19.2]

Disbanding

If all your members are killed and you cannot afford to resurrect them, your clan may be effectively out of the game. In this case, you may need to delete your clan and start over.

A disbanded clan's name is recorded to prevent immediate re-creation with the same name. If your BBS participates in an InterBBS league, deletion notifications are sent to all other villages.

[19.3]

News

The game keeps a daily news log of significant events: clan fights, new clans, ruler changes, empire attacks, and more. Access the news from the pre-game menu:

KeyAction
TToday's news.
YYesterday's news.
TIP: Check the news regularly to keep up with village politics and see who has been fighting whom.
[19.4]

Viewing Stats

Press V from almost any menu to view your clan's current statistics. This shows:

  • Your clan name, points, and ranking.
  • Each member's name, level, HP, SP, status, and equipment.
  • Your clan's gold.
  • Your empire summary.
  • Your alliance memberships.

Use this often to keep track of your members' health, equipment, and training point balances.
