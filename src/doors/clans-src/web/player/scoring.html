Point Values

Your clan earns or loses points based on your actions. Points determine your ranking on the scoreboard.

ActionPoints
Win a clan fight+20 points
Lose a clan fight-15 points
Run from a clan fight-20 points
Win a monster fight+10 points
Lose or run from a monster fight-10 points
Become village ruler+20 points
Abdicate the throne-100 points
TIP: Running from a clan fight is penalized more harshly than losing. Standing your ground, even in defeat, costs you fewer points than fleeing.

These point changes appear in the daily news, so all players can track the rise and fall of the clans.
