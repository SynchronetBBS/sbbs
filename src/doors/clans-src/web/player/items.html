Item Types

Items in The Clans fall into several categories:

TypeDescription
WeaponsIncrease your attack damage.
ArmorIncrease your defense.
ShieldsProvide additional protection.
ScrollsSingle-use spells that can be read during combat.
Spell BooksPermanently teach a spell to one member.
MiscellaneousVarious other items with special properties.

Your clan can hold up to 30 items total.

[8.2]

Equipping Items

After purchasing or finding items, you must equip them on your members for them to take effect. Each member can have one weapon, one set of armor, and one shield equipped at a time. Unequipped items remain in your clan's shared inventory.

[8.3]

Quality

The quality of items available at the market varies day to day. Market quality fluctuates between average, good, very good, and excellent. Better quality means better stats on the items for sale, but also higher prices.
