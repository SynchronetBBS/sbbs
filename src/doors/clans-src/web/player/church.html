THE CHURCH
11.1Overview 11.2Services 11.3Resurrection Costs
[11.1]

Overview

The church provides healing and spiritual services. It is only available if the village has a church built. Higher church levels unlock more powerful services.

The commands are:

KeyAction
MAttend mass (once per day).
PPray (once per day).
BBlessing -- fully restores your entire clan's SP (requires church level 2 or higher, once per day).
URevive unconscious -- resurrect unconscious members (requires church level 2 or higher).
DResurrect dead -- raise dead members (requires church level 3 or higher).
VView stats.
/Chat with villagers in the church.
QReturn to the main menu.
[11.2]

Services

Attending mass and praying are daily rituals that may grant small blessings or bonuses. Each can be done once per day.

The blessing (B) fully restores all present members' spell points to their maximums. This is very useful when your clan has exhausted its SP during combat.

Reviving unconscious members (U) and resurrecting dead members (D) cost gold from your clan's vault. The cost depends on the member's level.

[11.3]

Resurrection Costs

The cost to restore a fallen member depends on their level:

ServiceCost
Revive Unconscious(Level + 1) × 300 gold per member.
Resurrect Dead(Level + 1) × 750 gold per member.
Example: Reviving a level-4 unconscious member costs 1,500 gold. Resurrecting a level-4 dead member costs 3,750 gold.

The number of resurrections available per day depends on the church level. Build a higher-level church to restore more members each day.
