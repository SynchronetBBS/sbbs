[16.1]

Overview

Non-player characters (NPCs) wander through the village and can be found in various locations. They may have useful information, offer quests, or simply provide flavor to the world.

[16.2]

Talking to NPCs

When you chat with villagers (press /) in any location, you may encounter NPCs. You can talk to them about specific topics to learn things or trigger quest events.

NPCs wander between six zones: the church, the street, the market, the town hall, the training hall, and the mines. Not every NPC is in every zone at all times -- they move around during maintenance.

TIP: Check different locations if you are looking for a particular NPC. They move between zones during daily maintenance.
