3.1In-Game Commands
[3.1]

In-Game Commands

Once you enter the game, the main menu gives you access to all areas:

KeyAction
EEnter the Mines. Go underground to fight monsters.
MMarket. Buy and sell weapons, armor, shields, and scrolls.
CCommunications. Read and write mail, post public messages.
WWorld Travel. Travel to other villages (InterBBS only).
TTown Hall. Visit the village government and voting booth.
PEmpire. Manage your clan's empire -- buildings, troops, and strategy.
UChurch. Attend mass, pray, receive blessings, and resurrect fallen members.
HTraining Hall. Train your members' attributes and manage your roster.
AAlliances. Join or create alliances with other clans.
VView Stats. Display your clan's current statistics.
/Chat. Chat with other villagers in the street.
NNewbie Help. Display beginner tips.
!Delete Clan. Permanently delete your clan (confirmation required).
QQuit. Return to the pre-game menu.
The V (view stats), ? (redisplay menu), and Q (quit) commands are available in nearly every menu throughout the game.
