ALLIANCES
14.1Overview 14.2Creating and Joining
[14.1]

Overview

Alliances are groups of clans that band together for mutual benefit. Alliance members can coordinate their efforts in village politics and empire warfare.

[14.2]

Creating and Joining

Press A from the main menu to enter the alliance screen. You will see a list of existing alliances. Select one to view its members or request to join.

Each clan can create one alliance and belong to up to 5 alliances total. The game supports a maximum of 16 alliances.

To create a new alliance, select the option to form one and give it a name. Other players can then join your alliance through the alliance menu.
