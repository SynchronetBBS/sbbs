WORLD TRAVEL
17.1Overview 17.2Traveling
[17.1]

Overview

If the sysop has enabled InterBBS mode, your village is part of a larger league of villages hosted on different BBSes. World travel lets you move your clan between these villages.

Press W from the main menu to access the world travel menu:

KeyAction
OOther villages. View the list of villages in the league.
TTravel. Choose a village to travel to (not available on your first day).
SCurrent trip info. See your travel status.
HHelp. Information about village travel.
VView stats.
QReturn to the main menu.
[17.2]

Traveling

To travel, press T and select a destination village. Your clan data is packaged up and sent to the destination BBS during the next maintenance cycle. You will be able to play at the new village the following day.

WARNING: While traveling, you cannot play at your current village. Plan your travels carefully.

Travel is not available on your first day in the game. You must play at least one day locally before you can travel.
